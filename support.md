## 1. Contact

If you need help with the app, contact support at:

[pavelgedko@icloud.com](mailto:pavelgedko@icloud.com)

Support is available in English only.

## 2. What We Can Help With

You can contact support about:

- app features not working as expected;
- crashes, freezes, failed generation results, or other technical issues;
- subscription restore problems;
- questions related to privacy requests or data deletion; and
- general feedback related to the app's behavior.

## 3. What To Include In Your Message

To help investigate the issue faster, please include:

- a short description of what happened;
- the steps you took before the issue occurred;
- your device model;
- your iOS version;
- the app version;
- screenshots or screen recordings, if available; and
- any relevant purchase or restore details, if the issue is billing-related.

## 4. Subscriptions and Refunds

Subscriptions are billed and managed by Apple through the App Store.

You can manage or cancel your subscription through your Apple Account settings. Refund requests for App Store purchases are handled by Apple and are subject to Apple's policies.

If the issue is related to restoring premium access inside the app, you can still contact support at the email above.

## 5. Privacy Requests

If you want to request access to, deletion of, or other action regarding your personal data, email:

[pavelgedko@icloud.com](mailto:pavelgedko@icloud.com)

Please use a subject line such as `Privacy Request` so the request can be processed more efficiently.
